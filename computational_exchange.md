## Computational Exchange on Wednesday

The **Computational Exchange on Wednesday** (ExMi) is an in-person coding club at the Department of Media and Communication at LMU Munich. We meet **two to three times per semester**, always on **Wednesday at 12:00**, in **Akademiestraße 7, 6th floor**.

Not every session follows a fixed topic — you are welcome to bring your own computational questions, error messages, or work-in-progress. We are open topic-wise and always happy to see new faces. If you live in Munich or are visiting and interested in Computational Social Science, feel free to drop by or [get in touch](mailto:p.parschan@lmu.de).

**Digital attendance:** [Zoom link](https://lmu-munich.zoom.us/j/93767121254?pwd=WnJSQjloam1MeGVUbTRJOXIzWGFaUT09)
